About us

We are accepting CS:GO skins, keys and cases as tickets for a lottery. The more you deposit, more tickets you get - higher chance to win. After a certain amount of skins is collected the winner is choosen which will receive all items in the current round except around 5%-10% of value (service fee). You can't take your deposited items back.

IMPORTANT!
Your profile and inventory needs to be public to be able to deposit items.
Deposit items which are not listed on community market only at your own risk (the price is hard-coded and patterns/looks do not change value)
Souvenir skins can't be deposited!

What we are

What's all this for?

What if you could use your items (including useless stuff like cases or randomly dropped items), to participate in a lottery? This is what we aim to create: a lottery using the items from Counter Strike: Global Offensive.

What's special about us?

You might be thinking we're just copycats, but consider this: CSGOJackpot, the most popular CS:GO chance-based webpage out there, only allows $10 or higher deposits. This is unfair to players with less resources, so we are offering lottery tickets at a price every single CS:GO player can afford.

How do you know you can trust us?

You don't. There is no way we can assure you of our good intentions, however many promises we make in text. You try the system out with a very low value items. When the winner is announced, if it's not you, check the person's inventory. The current round items should be there when the winner accepts offer with won items.
